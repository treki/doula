From Section One: Vision:

I mostly live in the world of motherhood; I have taught preschool and Kindermusik, supported women as their doula, and mentored at our local MOPS group. I often see beautiful women pouring themselves into their much-loved little ones. Within this world of motherhood, there is so much discussion about seeing the uniqueness of each child, helping them discover their own strengths and gifts, and encouraging them to grow in their own precious way. However, I also see that as Mommas, we can tend to lose ourselves in the tasks of caring for children and running homes. We focus on the development of the children, and lose sight of the fact that our Father also made each of us a precious creation, reflecting Him in an individual way.
It doesn't have to be this way.
While our hands are so busy with the tasks of motherhood, I strongly believe that we can still keep sight of, and incorporate, the gifts and personality God has given us. I designed this workbook to be a tool to help.Living a life of purpose and intention is never an accident. This workbook is carefully designed to help you find vision and set goals, guiding you as you think through your God-given gifts and callings. Included are sections to develop a personal life plan and record progress each month, to aid you in keeping the focus on pursuing the things that are truly important. With clear, lighthearted artwork and the voice of an encouraging friend, you now have tools at your disposal to help you be more proactive than ever before in living your own beautiful life
This book is a result of my search for a set of tools to help me live my life with intention and purpose, having a clear vision of the gifts that God has given me, and balance in how I share them with the world.
So many other goal-and-priority setting books end up either too left-brained and uncreative, or far too scattered and incoherent for my needs. This workbook has been designed to have a gentle, purposeful order, while keeping a soft beauty that is welcoming and clean, instead of cluttered and busy.
I have incorporated the best tools that I found during my searches. These include ways of developing over-arching vision for my life, while defining my priorities clearly. I have utilized the best of the life-planning theories, and created a way to check in with myself on a regular basis to evaluate how well purposeful growth is occurring in those priorities.
The years that I have worked to develop--and use--this system have been my most fulfilling and purposeful. My prayer is that it will help you do the same.
